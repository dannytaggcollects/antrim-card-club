About the club

Cards bring us together.

Antrim Card Club is a welcoming place to share the hobby, grow your collection and meet fellow fans from across the area.

More than a card meet

We’re building a genuine local community around collecting. The club creates an easy-going space to browse, talk, trade and learn — without pressure or pretence.

Whether you’re chasing a favourite character, completing a set, collecting for the artwork or just getting started, you’ll find people who share the excitement.

Our approach

Friendly. Fair. Fun.

We want every collector to feel comfortable at the club. Respectful trading and a family-friendly atmosphere come first.
